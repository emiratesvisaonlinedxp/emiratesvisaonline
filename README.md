How to Check Emirates PNR Status — Quick & Easy Guide
Traveling with Emirates? Your PNR (Passenger Name Record) is the key to tracking your booking — from confirmation and seat details to flight updates and check-in information. Checking your PNR status before departure helps avoid surprises and stay informed about your flight.

**What Is Emirates PNR Status?**

A PNR is a unique code (usually six alphanumeric characters) that stores your booking details with Emirates. It shows your reservation’s current status — whether confirmed, waitlisted, or updated due to flight changes.

**How to Check Your Emirates PNR Status**

Here are the easiest ways to view your Emirates PNR status:

**1. Check Online via Emirates Official Website**

Go to the official Emirates website.

Navigate to Manage or Flight Status.

Enter your PNR code and last name.

Click Submit to see your current status and details.

This method gives you real-time flight info directly from the airline.

**2. Use the Emirates Mobile App**

Download the official Emirates app on your smartphone:

✔ Available for both iOS and Android
✔ Lets you check your PNR, itinerary, and flight updates anytime
✔ Provides push notifications if your flight changes or gets delayed

3. Contact Customer Support

If you’re having trouble online or need help understanding your PNR status, Emirates customer support can assist. Just call their helpline and provide your PNR code.

**Why You Should Always Check Your PNR Status**

Checking your Emirates PNR status before traveling helps you:

Verify your seat and itinerary
Confirm flight timings and terminals
Track changes or delays
Prepare documents and check-in early

Staying updated gives you peace of mind and a smoother travel experience.

**Quick Tips**

Save your PNR code in your phone or travel app
Check status 24-48 hours before departure
Review any airline notifications or alerts
